# Independent Study at Osaka University - SANKEN Lab

Welcome to the repository documenting my independent study program at Osaka University, specifically within the SANKEN (The Institute of Scientific and Industrial Research) laboratory.

## Overview
During this program, I am continuing and advancing my Master's (S2) thesis research. This study provides a valuable opportunity to deepen my research with international exposure, expert guidance, and state-of-the-art resources at Osaka University.

## Key Research Focus & Insights
My research here focuses on enhancing neural network architectures and training methodologies. The core insights and areas of exploration during my time at SANKEN include:

*   **Curriculum Learning:** Exploring and applying curriculum learning strategies to improve the training stability and performance of my models. By structuring the training data from simpler to more complex examples, I aim to achieve better convergence and generalization in my thesis model.
*   **Refining Attention Mechanisms:** Investigating the interplay between different attention mechanisms. Specifically, I am working on refining **Cross-Attention** by integrating or supplementing it with **Self-Attention**. This approach is intended to capture richer contextual representations and improve the model's ability to align and process information from complex data sources.

## About SANKEN
[SANKEN (The Institute of Scientific and Industrial Research)](https://www.sanken.osaka-u.ac.jp/en/) at Osaka University is a premier research institute fostering interdisciplinary research and technological innovation.
